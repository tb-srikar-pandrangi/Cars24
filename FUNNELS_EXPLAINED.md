# CARS24 Growth Operator - Funnels Explained

## Overview
The CARS24 Growth Operator monitors customer acquisition across **4 business funnels**. Each funnel represents a different business line with unique customer journeys and conversion paths.

---

## The 4 Funnels

### 1. **SELL Funnel** 🚗
**What it does**: Drives customers to **sell their used cars** to CARS24.

**Customer Journey**:
- User visits CARS24 website/app
- Enters car details (model, year, condition, mileage)
- Gets instant valuation
- Books inspection appointment
- Car gets inspected and evaluated
- **Conversion**: Sells car to CARS24

**Key Metrics**:
- **CPL (Cost per Lead)**: Cost to get one user to submit car details (~₹1,200-1,400)
- **CMA (Cost per Appointment)**: Cost to get one inspection appointment (~₹2,300-2,500)
- **Target**: High volume of sellers at low customer acquisition cost

**Example Campaigns**:
- "Sell Your Car in 10 Minutes" (Meta/Google)
- "Get Instant Valuation" (Facebook)
- "Quick Car Evaluation" (YouTube)

---

### 2. **BUY Funnel** 🛒
**What it does**: Drives customers to **purchase used cars** from CARS24 inventory.

**Customer Journey**:
- User browses CARS24 used car inventory
- Filters by price, brand, location
- Views detailed car listings
- Schedules test drive appointment
- Test drives car
- **Conversion**: Completes purchase with finance/cash

**Key Metrics**:
- **CPL (Cost per Lead)**: Cost to get one car listing view/inquiry (~₹1,350-1,500)
- **CMA (Cost per Appointment)**: Cost to schedule one test drive (~₹3,700-3,900)
- **Target**: High-intent buyers with higher CMA (justified by higher transaction value)

**Example Campaigns**:
- "Find Your Perfect Used Car" (Google Shopping)
- "Best Deals on Pre-Owned Vehicles" (Meta)
- "Certified Pre-Owned Cars at Best Prices" (YouTube)

---

### 3. **FINANCE Funnel** 💳
**What it does**: Drives customers to take **car loans/financing** from CARS24.

**Customer Journey**:
- User applies for car loan online
- Provides financial documents
- Gets loan approval in minutes
- Loan amount determined
- **Conversion**: Closes loan application

**Key Metrics**:
- **CPL (Cost per Lead)**: Cost to get one loan application (~₹900-1,000)
- **CMA (Cost per Appointment)**: Cost for one loan approval meeting (~₹2,000-2,100)
- **Target**: Lower CPL, streamlined digital process, high conversion rate

**Example Campaigns**:
- "Quick Car Loan Approval" (Search ads)
- "Finance Your Car in Minutes" (Meta)
- "Instant Loan Disbursement" (SMS/Email)

---

### 4. **SERVICES Funnel** 🔧
**What it does**: Drives customers to use **after-sales services** (maintenance, insurance, extended warranty, spare parts).

**Customer Journey**:
- Customer owns a CARS24 vehicle
- Needs servicing/maintenance/insurance
- Books service appointment
- Completes service/buys products
- **Conversion**: Service booking or product purchase

**Key Metrics**:
- **CPL (Cost per Lead)**: Cost to get one service inquiry (~₹1,450-1,600)
- **CMA (Cost per Appointment)**: Cost to schedule one service appointment (~₹3,200-3,400)
- **Target**: Recurring revenue from existing customer base

**Example Campaigns**:
- "Genuine Parts & Service" (Retargeting)
- "Extended Warranty Coverage" (Email/SMS)
- "Insurance Addon Protection" (In-app)

## Dashboard Insights

### What You're Looking At:

**Live Feed**: Shows real-time performance of all campaigns across all 4 funnels.

**Campaign Performance Snapshot** (KPI Cards):
- **Impressions**: Total ad views across all funnel campaigns
- **Click Rate**: % of people who clicked your ads
- **Leads**: Total qualified leads generated
- **Cost/Lead**: Average cost to acquire one lead

**Funnel Health**: 4-column view showing health of each funnel.
- View avg CMA per funnel
- See all campaigns for each funnel
- Identify which funnel is performing well vs. needs optimization

**Geographic Performance**: Which cities/regions drive the best results.
- Cities with lowest CMA = most efficient
- Severity color shows health status
- Helps allocate budgets to top-performing geographies

**Funnel Diagnostics**: AI detects conversion bottlenecks
- Where customers are dropping off
- Suggested fixes and optimizations
- Real-time alerts for critical issues

**Recommended Actions**: System suggests budget reallocations
- Shift budget from low-performing to high-performing campaigns
- Increase bids in top-converting cities
- Pause underperforming campaign variants

## Key Performance Indicators to Monitor

### Healthy Campaign Metrics:
- **CPL**: ₹900-1,500 (varies by funnel)
- **CMA**: ₹2,000-4,000 (varies by funnel)
- **Click Rate**: 2-5% (industry average)
- **Conversion Rate**: 3-8% (lead to appointment)

### Red Flags 🚨:
- CPL increasing > 20% week-over-week → Audience saturation
- CMA increasing > 15% → Quality drop
- Click Rate < 1% → Ad creative fatigue
- Geographic shift → Budget leakage to expensive regions
